Rooms for Secrets | Antonio Syxty | Teatro Litta Milano

Sound design for performance by Antonio Syxty at Teatro Litta, Milano, 2019.

Rooms For Secrets is a project of environmental performance created by Antonio Syxty who occupies an entire floor of an early twentieth century building with the intent of governing the public between false values and unexpected interpretations.

With the collaboration of Susanna Baccari. Text by Michele Zaffarano. Sound design by Robert Jack.
